About Us - RTA

Our History

The Road and Transport Authority (RTA) was established in 2005 to oversee all transportation-related activities in our city.

Since then, we have been dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for residents and visitors by providing safe, efficient, and sustainable transportation solutions.

Our Future Vision

At RTA, we envision a future where transportation is seamless, accessible to all, and environmentally friendly.

We are committed to embracing innovation and technology to achieve this vision, ensuring that our city remains a global leader in transportation excellence.
